Creamfields Music Festival, England

Creamfields Festival

Creamfields Festival is an electronic music festival hosted by event club promoter Cream, with it's UK edition taking place August Bank Holiday weekend annually in Daresbury, Cheshire. The Festival attracts over 280,000 visitors across the weekend. Creamfields is an electronic dance music festival series founded and organised by British club promoter Cream, with its UK edition taking place on August Bank Holiday weekend, with a number of international editions held across various territories worldwide. Tickets for Creamfields can be purchased from $180 and up.

Acts + Info

Creamfields attracts every type of music lover, from Hardstyle to Tech House, they have it all. The festival is renowned for their world famous "Steel Yard" stage, where the worlds top Techno artists perform, for example Nina Kravis, Black Coffee and Fatboy Slim to name a few. The festivals main stage is called the Arc Stage and is a massive superstructure with state of the art pyrotechnics and visuals. Click Here to visit Creamfields website!
