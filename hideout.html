Hideout Music Festival, Croatia

Hideout Festival

Hideout Festival is an annual electronic music festival held in Zrce, Croatia on the island of Pag. The first festival was in 2011, and landed on The Guardian's list of the best European festivals of 2011, and since then, has sold out every year to date. The festival is held from the 20th to the 24th of June and houses more than 30,000 attendees across the weekend.

Acts + Info

Hideout attracts every Techno and Tech house lover around the world and has one of the best lineups out there. The festival has amazing artists every year.. Camelphat, Alan Fitzpatrick, Rebuke and Nina Kraviz to name a few. Click Here to visit Hideouts website!
