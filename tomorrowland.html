Tomorrowland Music Festival, Belgium

Tomorrowland Festival

Tomorrowland is one of the, if not the most renowned music festivals in the world. It takes place in Boom, Belgium and welcomed more than 400,000 visitors from over 80 countries across 2 weekends in 2019. The first weekend takes place the 19th-21st of July and the second weekend taking place the 26th-29th of July. Over 40,000 Tomorrowland visitors stay in "Dreamville", Tomorrowland's very own campsite for the festival and the remainder of the visitors would stay in allocated accomodation etc. Getting tickets for Tomorrowlandis like gold dust, tickets typically sell out in minutes due to demand, with tickets starting at 400$ and up.

Acts + Info

Tomorrowland houses all types of electronic music genres, from techno to future house music. Typically, the festival sells out in minutes due to its popular demand and ridiculous lineups. Usually all of the A list DJ's perform at the festival, as it is literally the home of DJ's. The likes of Dimitri Vegas & Like Mike, Martin Garrix, Alesso, Eric Prydz are performing every single year without fail.
